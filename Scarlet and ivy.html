"The Scarlet and Ivy" series by Sophie Cleverly is a middle-grade mystery series set in an eerie English boarding school. The story follows twin sisters Scarlet and Ivy Gray, and it combines elements of mystery, suspense, friendship, and courage.






book one.

Ivy, I pray that it's you reading this. And if you are, well, I suppose you're the new me... When shy Ivy's troublemaking twin Scarlet vanishes from Rookwood boarding school, Ivy is invited to "take her place." But when Ivy arrives, she discovers the school's true intention; she has to pretend to be Scarlet. She must think like Scarlet, act like Scarlet, become Scarlet. What on earth happened to the real Scarlet, and why is the school trying to keep it a secret? Luckily for Ivy, Scarlet isn't about to disappear without a fight. She's left pieces of her journal carefully hidden all over the school for Ivy to find. Ivy's going to figure out what happened to Scarlet. She's got to. But the staff of Rookwood is always watching, and they'll do anything to keep their secrets buried‚Ä¶
















book two.

At the bidding of their cold-hearted stepmother, twin sisters Scarlet and Ivy are sentenced to board for a year at Rookwood School. The headmaster is cruel, the hallways are drafty, and there seems to be a thief afoot. When the finger of suspicion is pointed at Scarlet, she'll do whatever it takes to clear her name--including some late-night detective work. But in the darkness of Rookwood, mysteries of the past come to light. The walls are talking of secrets past, and it's up to Scarlet and Ivy to listen to their story...
















book three.

Scarlet and Ivy return to Rookwood School after the previous headmaster, Mr. Bartholomew, is gone. A new ballet teacher, Madame Zelda, arrives after Miss Finch disappears, and strange occurrences begin to plague the school. Poison-pen letters and accidents threaten the students, and the twins must uncover who is behind the dangerous events.
















book four.

The fourth book in the Scarlet and Ivy series, "The Lights Under the Lake," follows the twins on a school trip to Shady Pines Hotel on Lake Seren. Mysterious events plague the guests, and the twins investigate rumors of restless spirits and a flooded graveyard linked to the hotel's past.
















book five.

The fifth book in the Scarlet and Ivy series, "The Curse in the Candlelight", introduces a new enigmatic student, Ebony, at Rookwood School. Scarlet and Ivy become suspicious of Ebony when a prank on All Hallow's Eve goes wrong, and she's blamed. The twins investigate, uncovering secrets and questioning whether Ebony is truly dangerous or if something else is at play.
















book six.

The sixth and final book in the Scarlet and Ivy series, "The Last Secret," finds the twins returning to Rookwood School after the holidays to discover it's in danger and under the control of the son of a previous, evil headmaster. Scarlet and Ivy must confront both past and present enemies to save the school and uncover a final, potentially devastating secret.
